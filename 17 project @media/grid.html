COURSE OVERVIEW

join the Specialist Game Development Program

The PG Professional Program in Game Development at ICAT is a 1-year, full-time program that provides extensive knowledge in programming, which helps students to build highly functional products. The program presents platform-specific knowledge on game building, thus enabling students to build cross-platform products. It trains students to work with Game Engines to develop high quality games in short time. Further, the program offers intense practice to take up an exciting career as a Game Programmer, AI/UI Programmer, Tool Developer, Level Designer, and much more.

Why Game Development?

Get Ready to Take Up an In-Demand Career

India stands second in digital adoption growth rate, which along with high internet usage, increased affordability of smartphones, emergence of e-sports, and adoption of new technologies like AR and VR have compelled a high growth rate in the Indian Gaming industry. Also, the number of genres in digital games today is very high — from shooter games, action games, and sports games to multiplayer online games, real-time strategy games, and horror games. Such vast gaming genres along with huge growth in the industry and high expectation for something different in every new game compel the need for highly innovative game development professionals. Further, the leaders of the gaming industry establish their development centres in India, widening the opportunities for skilled game development professionals. Just pitch in to have a flourishing career.
